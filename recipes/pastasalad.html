Pasta Salad

Pasta Salad-the perfect summer side dish

This dish features fresh veggies, a tangy dressing, and filling pasta.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz pasta of choice
  • 8 oz cucumber
  • 8 oz tomato
  • 8 oz pepperoni
  • 12 oz feta cheese
  • 4 oz can of black olives
  • greek dressing to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. While waiting for water to boil, chop veggies to bite size pieces.
  3. Drain olives and leave to the side.
  4. Once water is boiling, add a pinch of salt and pasta. Cook according to box instructions.
  5. Drain pasta and allow to cool.
  6. After pasta has cooled, combine ingredients in a large bowl. Add greek dressing to taste.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Enjoy!
